General policy and terms

1. Registration Policy

We have general policies and regulations for students, which we look forward to when you sign up and take our courses.

2. Tuition refund and guarantee policy

For some reason you are unable to continue taking our courses, we will review and refund/insinge your tuition fees:

2.1. Refund Policy

  • Before the first day of the course, you will be refunded 90% of your tuition fee (please present your own ID card or photo ID.)
  • Within one week after the first day of class: please contact the Enroll Counseling department directly to make a request, the Department of Education will settle and refund your tuition (the percentage will vary according to the expected duration of the class.)
  • One week after the first day of class: we are sorry, you will not be able to settle tuition refunds.

2.2 Tuition Preservation

  • You need to contact the Enroll Counseling Department to make the request and the Department of Education will resolve for you to be transferred to the next course and the tuition difference
  • Your course guarantee period is up to 6 months from the time you make the Request.

3.2. 3.2. You may not copy content csc.edu.vn to a third system without our consent.
