Information Privacy Policy

1.Purpose and scope of collection

The main data collection on the website includes: full name, email, phone of students when registering online. This is the information that we need students to provide when submitting information that needs advice or want to register for the course, and for us to contact the student to confirm the benefits for students.

2. Scope of use of information

We use the information provided by students to:

  • Contact students to confirm the course they want to register;
  • Provide information about courses and schedules to students;
  • Send an email introducing the new course, preferential policies and necessary information to take care of students;
  • Contact and resolve with students in special cases;
  • At the request of judicial authorities, including: Procuracies, courts and investigating police agencies related to certain law-breaking acts of certain students.

3. Time of information storage

The student's personal data will be stored until a request is requested by the board to cancel. Remaining in all cases personal information students will be secured on our server.

4. People or organizations who have access to personal information

Subjects have access to personal information of students in one of the following cases:

  • Our Staff, Our Teachers
  • Our partners and undertake to use this information only for the purpose of benefiting students (recruitment, employment, workshops,...) and must not be used for other purposes without our consent.

5. Address of the unit collecting and managing personal information

CENTER FOR INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Address: 227 Nguyen Van Cu Street, District 5, HCMC

Hotline: 028 3835 1056

6. Means and tools for users to access and edit their personal data

Students can update and adjust their personal information by contacting the Enroll Counseling department directly, or by emailing tuvan@csc.hcmus.edu.vn to do so

7. Commitment to condidentiality of customers' personal information

Do not use, transfer, provide to any third party personal information of students without permission from students.

In the event that the server stores student information that has been hacked resulting in the loss of student's personal data, we will be responsible for notifying the case to the investigating authorities in a timely manner and notifying the student.

We expect our students to provide relevant personal information such as full name, date of birth, place of birth, email, phone, ID,...., and be responsible for the accuracy of the above information. We are not responsible for, nor will we resolve any complaints relating to the rights of our students, if the personal information provided by the student upon initial registration is inaccurate.
