[Career duidance] Learn design: Easy to find a job - Attractive income

Short training time, low cost, being able to find ajob anywhere are the advantages that make a large number of young people today choose Graphic Design as the path to future career development.

In an age of booming information, universities or colleges are no longer the only way to succeed, a survey has shown that the higher the degree, the more likely it is to be unemployed, because sometimes the major you choose is not necessarily suitable for you, the job market is difficult to compete with only a University degree is not enough. Today's employers often value practical work experience and most importantly, the spirit of work, do you really love your job? Many CEOs think that recent graduates are not prepared for the job they apply for, because besides the degree you need to have passion.

Seeing the elements on many young people who have found their own way, you love creativity and passion for design work, have come to the Multimedia Graphic Designer program to create a solid stepping stone, accumulate enough skills ready to become a professional Designer. There have been cases where you have abandoned your engineering degree, bachelor's degree to pursue and venture into the field of Graphic Design, where you live fully every creative day every day.

Careers come from a passion for creativity, focusing on skills over degree



How do I know if I'm a good fit for Graphic Design?

Do you love pictures, color?

You are fascinated by beautiful, eye-catching advertising banners, if encountered on the road you often stop to see. When reading books, magazines or surfing Facebook, you often pay attention to beautiful images, unique fonts, color combinations ... You have potential in design!


Do you like drawing?

The job of a designer is mostly drawing, being able to draw hands or use software, to become a professional designer you need to enjoy drawing. No need for you to draw beautifully, no need for you to have hand flowers, just you really like. Many of you are afraid of this job because "I paint is not beautiful", but do you know that in reality aptitude only accounts for 5% of the level of success in the profession. Through the program, you will be trained and learn more skills to improve yourself every day. So don't worry if drawing isn't your forer, as long as you really love it, you'll do it.


Learn new things

Graphic design mobilizes according to the trend of the era, according to the needs of the market. You need the ability to adapt quickly, learn, not be afraid to change and update new design trends to keep up with the flow of the industry.

Design has never been a idle job, you have to innovate yourself every day, each product is a new experience that you have to conquer. Unique and different is what you want people to remind you of, if you love new creative things, welcome you to join the future Designer team.

Study Design into a career in just 1 year

Why is the Multimedia Graphic Designer program right for you?

The program is designed specifically for high school graduates who are oriented to pursue and embark on the path of becoming a professional designer.

With only 12 months of training, you are fully equipped with skills and practical experience ready to apply for any professional Graphic Design position.A variety of specials as well as proactive learning time are the highlights of the Specialist program.

The program includes 3 main semesters and graduation projects:

Term 1 - 2D Graphic Design: Helps you master 2D Design tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Corel and applied art knowledge in Design. This is an indispensable foundation step of any Designer.

Term 2 - Advanced Graphic design: Intensive training professional graphic design, development and application of ideas to actual products. Master the principle of using colors, compositions, Typography, photography, Wacom skills ... Find out what a Brand Identity System is, and make a proper and unique identity.

Term 3 - You choose one of 4 majors: Web Design, Interior 3D Design, Motion Graphic, Digital Painting

Job oppurtunities in Graphic Design

Your biggest concern when deciding to study a certain major is the job opportunities after school.

With the trend of integration, many companies were born leading to increasingly fierce commercial competition. To build a brand image regardless of the business, big or small, any business needs to recuit Graphic Designers. That is why the graphic design major with many job opportunities and is attracting a large number of creative young people to participate.

Some of the positions you can apply for after completing the Graphic Designer program include: Graphic Designer; Web Designer; Brand designer; Motion graphic designer; Interior designer; Digital Artist; Illustrating magazine books... in enterprises, advertising companies - perseverance, design companies, studios, publishing houses, studios ... or become a Freelancer who works wherever you want with an extremely competitive and attractive income.
