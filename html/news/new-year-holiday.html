[Notice] New Year Holiday Schedule 2020

Greetings to Teachers, Teachers and Students of Information Technology Center

A new year opens up many new opportunities that are coming very close, to help teachers and students be more proactive in arranging time, the Center would like to announce and schedule a welcome break in 2020 as follows:

New Year Holiday Schedule:

  • All classes are given 1 day off - Wednesday 01/01/202
  • Thursday - January 2nd,2020 classes return to school


Lunar New Year holiday schedule:

  • All classes will end by Friday - January 17, 2020(i.e December 23rd of the lunar calendar)
  • Classes will start on Wednesday - February 12, 2020(i.e January 19th of the lunar calendar)
  • The center is open from Thursday to January 30, 2019 (6th day of tet)


We wish you, teachers and all students a new year of Heath and Well-being and Success!
